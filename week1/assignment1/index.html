I have a pet cat named Sally

We found her looking scared-y

In a petsmart in Spokane

where we used to live, so dreary!

She won't scratch you, but she'll bite
when you rub her tummy, so fine

she'll chase a squirrel or two
but will run away from baby brother, so true!
